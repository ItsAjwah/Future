Company Introduction

Reign Corporation is one of the leading exporter of Himalayan salt products, Marble products, Sandstone, Minerals products and Rice in Pakistan. Our prime focus is to deliver quality products on competitive prices and send timely shipments.

Reign Corporation directly sources Himalayan Rock Salts from Himalayan salt mines in Pakistan therefore the products we produce are made from the finest quality Himalayan salt. We facilitate our customers for customization from products to their packaging.
